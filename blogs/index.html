EDUCATION-FINANCIAL VIEW

Everything costs money in today’s competitive world.It’s high time that each parent and student view education as an investment asset,where the parent (or investors) should get a much better return on investment (ROI as spoken in finance) in terms of knowledge,skills,grooming or most importantly a better qualified job placement of the student.

This hails for some informed set of choices to be made.
Here are some basic DO’s:

  1. Average placement vs fees in total.
  2. A research into quality of companies coming for on-campus placements
  3. Look into your specialization faculty of the college.
  4. Connecting with college recent alumni’s,it will help you analyze the industrial trend changes and shaping.

The education business should be taken seriously with every penny invested should add value.

Now with the advent of startup era, there are huge number of opportunities being generated everyday, you just need to have the skills and the relevant insight of the industry domain they are in. These informed steps taken along with your realized interests and strengths, will make you thrive in life above the all others.

MARKS ARE NOT YOU.

Higher marks doesn’t guarantee good career, better package or peace of mind.No matter how much hardwork you put in to get highest of them all.At the end,the one who figure’s out the perfect niche for himself only shines in respect to good job roles,money and a long fruitful career ahead.

So what could we do to discover our niche,our thing to do in life.Our aim should be one where our interests,personality and skills do align.By talking to them personally you will discover where you are lacking and how should you start on with the process of self-improvement.

Now,here comes the challenge to discover them,but from where:Reading wikipedia,seeing videos on youtube,talking to a cousin.The above we all have tried and got hands burned.The thing is,the job reality is quiet different and the guidance should come from one in position you desire.

There will be times,you get to know the cons of your desired career and you will drift very soon without wasting your crucial time of life.

YOU GET PAID IN REAL LIFE FOR YOUR SKILLS,NOT MARKS.

AN ENTRPRENUER?IT’S TOTALLY COOL

We are reaching towards an era where internet and technology have created millions of opportunity for people to grasp. And that is very less occupied until now.It’s totally fine if you think that despite of the degree’s earned,working for yourself is the best thing for you.

Sky is the limit for a self-starter in this era where people are willing to explore new products/concepts to make their life easier and comfortable. But being an entrepreneur,it’s a lot of risk,both of capital and time too.In your journey of making a difference to the world,you need to have a mentor who have already disrupted the market with his concepts and work ethics.Never make the same mistake again as done by others,your time is most important.

YOU SHOULD KNOW WHAT YOU ARE GETTING INTO.
